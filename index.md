## About

I am a doctoral candidate in **Texts and Technology** at the University of Central
Florida, bringing a strong interdisciplinary foundation in humanities scholarship,
a demonstrated commitment to student-centered pedagogy, and practical experience
designing inclusive, outcomes-driven courses across multiple modalities.

My work integrates digital humanities methods with cultural and media studies,
drawing genuine connections across art history, philosophy, religion, literature,
and music. My research centers on fan studies — how audiences perceive, remix, and
make meaning from adaptations and the industries that produce them. My dissertation,
*"Your Choices, Your Actions: Fan Perceptions of Superman Adaptations, Communities,
and Industry Practices,"* is anticipated August 2027.
